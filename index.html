  • Research Experience

    • (May.2015-Current) PhD Student & Research Assistant, Pervasive Communications Laboratory, Clemson University, SC, USA
      I work on the area of Cloud computing and hope to the solve the congestion control, flow scheduling and resource allocation in data center networks.

    • (Aug. 2013-Mar.2015) MS Student, Beijing Key Lab of Network System Architecture and Convergence, BUPT, Beijing
      I work on the area of intelligent optimization algorithms, such as Genetic Algorithm (GA), Quantum Genetic Algorithm (QGA), Particle Swarm Algorithm (PSO) and Q-Learning Algorithm, and put them into practice on wireless resource optimization in multiuser OFDM systems and HetNet. Based on these researches, I have authored one journal article (SCEI & EI), which is under review, and two international conference papers, which are published in ICT 2014 and WPMC 2014 respectively. In addition, in our study, data mining algorithm, like Decision Tree Algorithm, was used to divide users into coordinative ones and non-coordinative ones according the learning of the characteristics of users, such as SINR, Location, Packet Loss, Delay. Based on the research, I have coauthored an international conference paper "Network MIMO with decision tree classification in downlink OFDMA networks", which is published in ICT 2014.

    • (May 2013-Sept.2013) Android R&D, Beijing Novel-TV Media Technology Co., Ltd, Beijing.
      As a participant in the research and development (R&D) of an on-line video interactive system, I bore full responsibility for the R&D of the Android client, which contains three major functions: user login management, user camera selection management and video capture. In this system, users can capture their video by themselves, and transmit them immediately to the remote video server, from which the website users could watch them on website and other clients. This system can be used on campus, where students can share their lives with their families and friends in real time. Travellers can also share the beautiful sceneries with other friends. That is so cool. During this period, I have not only practiced my skills on Java programming, but also learned the fundamental knowledge of video sampling, encoding and decoding.

    • (Sept.2012-May 2013) MS Student, Beijing Key Lab of Network System Architecture and Convergence, BUPT, Beijing.
      • As a participant in the R&D of a video conference system, which supports video conference between PC users and Android users, I was responsible for the development of the user interface for the Android client, which contains the login management and user interface design. In this client, users can communicate with others over the connection in the server. During this period, my skills on Java programming in Android environment has been developed.
